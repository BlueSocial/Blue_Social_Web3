How do I program my Blue Smart Card?

To program your Blue Smart Card go to the hamburger menu and tap “Activate Blue Smart Card.” Then tap “Configure Now” and slide your Smart Card on the back of your phone to scan. Once complete you will receive a notification “Profile written to the NFC Tag successfully.”

How do I know if my Blue Smart Card is working?

You can test it by sliding it on the back of a compatible smartphone to see if it brings up your Blue profile.

How do I use my Blue Smart Card?

You can use it when networking and meeting other people by simply sliding it on the upper back of their smartphone.

How do I find my Facebook profile link?

Log into Facebook, then click on your name in the upper right part of the screen. Copy the Facebook Profile URL in the address bar of your browser.

How do I find my Linkedin profile link?

Click the Me icon at the top of your LinkedIn homepage. Click View profile then on your profile page, click edit public profile & URL on the right rail. You can copy and paste this link to share it with others.

What is beacon technology?

Beacons are used by businesses to advertise to users walking by using Bluetooth Low Energy. Users will be able to receive notifications of coupons and offers as they walk by a business.
